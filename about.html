What We Do:

A Dog's Brew is devoted to all dog lovers out there. We want to make our coffie shop as welcoming as possible to dogs. Think of this as a reward for man's best friend by serving them along with you!

Rules:

We have no complex rules regarding dogs, as this place is made for them! We only have two rules overall.

1. NO CATS ALLOWED

We have found it obvious about now, but Cats and Dogs do not mix well in the same place together. So you can't bring a cat into the resturant. And don't try to disguise it as a dog either! That will only end in a disaster.

2. Have a normal dog for pete's sake!

We never thought we would have to make that rule until that ONE family and their dog showed up. So as long as you are not any of them, or at least have a good dog, you can come.
